Recipe
Chicken fried rice
Step 1
Ingredients
1 pound chicken
3 cups cooked rice
2 Tbs sesame oil
1 small white onion, chopped
1 cup frozen peas and carrots, thawed
2-3 Tablespoons soy sauce
2 eggs
2 Tbsp chopped green onions
Cookware
Spatula
Knife
Chopping board
Wok
Cook and shred the chicken.
Preheat a wok to medium heat and Pour sesame oil in the bottom.
Substitute
Pan
Wok
Substitute
Olive oil
Canola oil
Sesame oil
Add white onion and peas and carrots and fry until tender.
Substitute
Onion
White onion
Slide the onion, peas and carrots to the side, and pour the beaten eggs onto the other side.
Using a spatula, scramble the eggs. Once cooked, mix the eggs with the vegetable mix.
Add the rice and chicken to the veggie and egg mixture.
Pour the soy sauce on top. Stir and fry the rice and veggie mixture until heated through and combined.
Substitute
It is optional.
Add chopped green onions if desired.
