Slice garlic and loosen eggs.
Mix a half cup of parmesan and loosen eggs. It will be sauce of carbonara.
Grease the frying pan by 3 spoons of olive oil and fry garlic and bacon at weak fire.
When bacon is shrunken, Put a half cup of water that boiled phedelini in pan.
When water is boiling, put phedelini in pan and fry it.
Combine phedelini and sauce, and then put pepper and parsley for deco.
